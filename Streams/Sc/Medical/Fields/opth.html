Who are Opthamalogists ?

Ophthalmology is a branch of medicine and surgery that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the eye.An ophthalmologist is a physician who specializes in ophthalmology. The credentials include a degree in medicine, followed by additional four to five years of residency training in ophthalmology. In India, after completing MBBS degree, postgraduate study in ophthalmology is required. The degrees are doctor of medicine, master of surgery, diploma in ophthalmic medicine and surgery, and diplomate of national board.
