Who are physician ?

A physician , medical practitioner , medical doctor, or simply doctor, is a professional who practices medicine, which is concerned with promoting, maintaining or restoring health through the study, diagnosis, prognosis and treatment of disease, injury, and other physical and mental impairments. Physicians may focus their practice on certain disease categories, types of patients, and methods of treatmen known as specialities. To work as a physician, you must first earn a bachelor's degree and then attend medical school for four additional years.
