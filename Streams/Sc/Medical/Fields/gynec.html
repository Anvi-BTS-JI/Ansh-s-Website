Who are gynecogists ?

Gynaecology or gynecology (see spelling differences) is the medical practice dealing with the health of the female reproductive system. Almost all modern gynaecologists are also obstetricians (see obstetrics and gynaecology). In many areas, the specialities of gynaecology and obstetrics overlap.The term means "the science of women". Its counterpart is andrology, which deals with medical issues specific to the male reproductive system. To work as a gynecologist you have to earn MBBS degree from a well-recognized medical institution. Appear the entrance test for taking admission in MD/MS course.
