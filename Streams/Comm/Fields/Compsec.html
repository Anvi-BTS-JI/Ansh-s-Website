Who are Company Secretaries ?

A Company Secretary is a senior position in a private sector company or public sector organisation. Also known as Compliance Officers, it is one of the positions that is a part of the key managerial personnel (which usually includes the CEO & CFO) of any company.Chartered Secretaries are employed as chairs, chief executives and non-executive directors, as well as executives and company secretaries.The CS (Company Secretary) is a 3 years professional course equivalent to the UG degree.
