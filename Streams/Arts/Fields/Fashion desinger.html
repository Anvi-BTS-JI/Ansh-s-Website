Who are fashion desingers ?

Fashion designers work in a variety of different ways in designing their pieces and accessories such as rings, bracelets and necklaces.To become a fashion designer there is training and certifications that will help you succeed in this profession. However, you don't need a college degree, although it would help you become more successful.To be hired as a fashion designer one needs to possess at least a bachelor's degree in the field of design and also the degree Bachelor's Visual arts.
