<center><h1>Who are civil servants ?</h1></center>
</body>
<p style='font-family:comic sans;font-size:xx-large;color:blue'>
The civil service is a collective term for a sector of government composed mainly of career civil servants hired on professional merit rather than appointed or elected, whose institutional tenure typically survives transitions of political leadership. A civil servant, also known as public servant, is a person employed in the public sector by a government department or agency for public sector . Civil servants work for central and state governments, and answer to the government.The candidate should have a Bachelor's degree in any discipline from any recognized University. How ever the arts is not the only field to become a civil interest. In India you must pass UPSC exams to become a civil servant but in different countries you must give different exams organized by government
